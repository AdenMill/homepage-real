Aden Millbaugh

I live in a vibrant household with my mom, dad, and my older sister, who is currently in her last year of college. Although she resides at school, our home remains a special place for her, filled with memories and familial love. We share a unique bond, often collaborating to care for our grandmothers. This responsibility has given me a wealth of experience in elder care, and I’ve grown to consider myself somewhat of an expert in that area.

Family is incredibly important to me, and I cherish spending time with both sides of my family. My circle of friends is small, but I appreciate the depth of those relationships. I find joy in the little things, like indulging in cartoons—believing wholeheartedly that you’re never too old for a good laugh.

When it comes to entertainment, my tastes lean toward action-packed movies and video games, with “Captain America: The First Avenger” holding the title of my favorite film. While I may not be the most avid reader, I find great pleasure in diving into comic books that combine engaging stories with stunning visuals.

Navigating life with ADHD can be a challenge, but I’ve managed to cultivate a sense of organization that helps me stay focused, even if I sometimes struggle. Oddly enough, while recalling specific information can be tricky, I can effortlessly recite lines from my favorite films. Procrastination is another hurdle I face, but it’s a part of who I am.

Music plays a significant role in my life, and you’ll often find me singing in the shower, lost in the rhythm of my favorite tunes. As I continue to grow, I remain committed to figuring out life, taking it one step at a time. Each moment, whether spent with family, friends, or indulging in a great movie, contributes to my journey of self-discovery and understanding.
